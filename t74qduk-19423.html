7. What is liability coverage?

Liability coverage refers to the portion of an auto insurance policy that pays for damages and injuries caused by the insured driver to others involved in an accident. It does not cover the insured driver’s own vehicle damages.

8. What is full coverage insurance?

Full coverage insurance typically includes both liability coverage and coverage for damages to your own vehicle. It provides greater protection but may come at a higher cost.
