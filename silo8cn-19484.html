1. The annual salary equivalent of $46 hourly:
To convert an hourly wage to an annual salary, multiply the hourly rate by the number of hours worked in a week, and then multiply that by 52 (the number of weeks in a year). This figure provides a solid foundation for financial planning and understanding the potential earnings associated with this wage.
