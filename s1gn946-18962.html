Jt City Girl Net Worth

Fact 1: Early Life and Musical Beginnings
Born on December 3, 1992, in Liberty City, Miami, JT had a challenging upbringing. She grew up in a neighborhood plagued by crime and poverty, but she didn’t let her circumstances define her future. Inspired by artists like Trina and Missy Elliott, JT developed a passion for music from a young age. She began writing lyrics and honing her rap skills, determined to make a name for herself in the industry.

Fact 2: Formation of City Girls
JT’s life took a significant turn when she met her future music partner, Yung Miami, in 2017. The duo quickly formed City Girls and caught the attention of Quality Control Music, a prominent record label.
