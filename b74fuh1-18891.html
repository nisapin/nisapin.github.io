Interesting Facts about Auto Insurance in Harrison, NJ:

2. Liability vs. Full Coverage: Liability insurance is the minimum coverage required by law in Harrison, NJ. It covers damages to another person’s vehicle or property if you are at fault in an accident. Full coverage, on the other hand, includes liability insurance and also covers damages to your own vehicle. While liability insurance is a cheaper option, full coverage provides more comprehensive protection.

These factors include your driving history, age, gender, type of vehicle, credit score, and the area where you live.
