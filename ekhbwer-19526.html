Best Year For Gmc Yukon


Best Year for GMC Yukon: 5 Interesting Facts

The GMC Yukon is a full-size SUV that has gained immense popularity over the years. With its powerful performance, spacious interior, and advanced technology, the Yukon has become a favorite choice for families and adventure enthusiasts alike. If you’re considering purchasing a GMC Yukon, it’s essential to know which year offers the best features and performance. In this article, we will discuss the best year for the GMC Yukon and provide you with five interesting facts about this remarkable vehicle.

1. The Best Year for GMC Yukon: 2019
When it comes to the best year for the GMC Yukon, the 2019 model stands out as an excellent choice. In this year, GMC introduced significant updates and enhancements to the Yukon, making it one of the most impressive SUVs on the market. The 2019 Yukon offers a powerful 6.2-liter V8 engine, capable of producing 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque. Additionally, it features a refined and luxurious interior, advanced safety features, and a smooth and comfortable ride.

2. Enhanced Safety Features
One of the key highlights of the GMC Yukon is its commitment to safety. The 2019 model comes equipped with a range of advanced safety features such as forward collision alert, lane keep assist, blind-spot monitoring, rear cross-traffic alert, and low-speed automatic emergency braking. These features enhance the overall safety of the vehicle, ensuring that you and your passengers are well-protected.

3. Spacious Interior
The GMC Yukon offers a spacious and comfortable interior, making it an ideal choice for families and individuals who require ample room for passengers and cargo. With seating for up to nine people, you can easily accommodate your entire family or a group of friends. The Yukon also provides impressive cargo space, with up to 94.7 cubic feet available when the rear seats are folded down.

4. Advanced Technology
GMC is known for incorporating advanced technology into its vehicles, and the Yukon is no exception. The 2019 model comes equipped with the GMC Infotainment System, which includes an 8-inch touchscreen, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, Bluetooth connectivity, and a Bose premium audio system. Additionally, the Yukon offers a rear-seat entertainment system, allowing passengers to enjoy their favorite movies or games during long journeys.

5. Towing Capabilities
If you’re looking for an SUV that can handle heavy towing, the GMC Yukon is an excellent choice. The 2019 model comes with a maximum towing capacity of up to 8,500 pounds when properly equipped. Whether you need to tow a boat, trailer, or other recreational vehicles, the Yukon delivers exceptional performance and capability.

14 Common Questions About the GMC Yukon:

1. How much does a GMC Yukon cost?
The price of a GMC Yukon varies depending on the year, trim level, and optional features. On average, a new GMC Yukon can range from $50,000 to $75,000.

2. What is the fuel efficiency of the GMC Yukon?
The fuel efficiency of the GMC Yukon varies depending on the engine and driving conditions. The 2019 Yukon with the 6.2-liter V8 engine has an estimated fuel economy of 14 mpg in the city and 23 mpg on the highway.

Yes, the GMC Yukon is available in both rear-wheel drive and four-wheel drive configurations.

Yes, GMC offers a hybrid version of the Yukon called the Yukon Hybrid. However, the hybrid model is not available in all years.

9. What is the warranty coverage for the GMC Yukon?
The GMC Yukon comes with a limited warranty that covers three years or 36,000 miles, whichever comes first.

10. How does the GMC Yukon compare to its competitors, such as the Chevrolet Tahoe?
The GMC Yukon and Chevrolet Tahoe are similar vehicles, as they share the same platform. However, the Yukon offers more upscale features and a slightly higher price point.

11. Can the GMC Yukon tow a travel trailer?
Yes, the GMC Yukon has the capability to tow a travel trailer. However, it is essential to ensure that the weight of the trailer is within the Yukon’s towing capacity.
