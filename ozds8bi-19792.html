Just like in every other state, liability insurance is mandatory in Virginia, including Stuarts Draft. While liability insurance is required, it may not provide sufficient coverage for your own vehicle. Full coverage insurance, on the other hand, offers comprehensive protection. This type of insurance not only covers the damages to the other party’s vehicle, but also covers your own expenses in case of an accident, theft, or damage caused by natural disasters. While full coverage insurance may cost more, it ensures peace of mind and can be worth the extra cost for some drivers.
